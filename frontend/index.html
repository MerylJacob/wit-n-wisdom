01

What it is

Wit & Wisdom is a conversational AI companion trained on all six of Jane Austen’s major novels. Ask about characters, themes, plot, relationships, or society, and receive answers grounded directly in Austen’s own words.

⸻
02

How it works

Powered by Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), all six novels are split into thousands of passages and stored as semantic vectors in ChromaDB. When you ask a question, the most relevant passages are retrieved and handed to a local Llama 3 model, which crafts a faithful, cited response. No data ever leaves your machine.

→ Your question is embedded into a vector
→ ChromaDB finds the closest novel passages
→ Llama 3 answers from those passages only
→ Sources are cited in every response
